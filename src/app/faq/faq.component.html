Product Support:


How do I get Product Support?

First go through the FAQ's to see if you find an answer to your question. You will also find valuable information under other sections. If your problem remains unsolved please raise a ticket with elaborate description.

How do I raise a ticket?

Go to homepage. Click on the plus button under the heading "Raise a ticket". Fill the details in the required format to enable the submit button. Finally, click on Submit.

How do I follow up a ticket?

After the ticket is raised, you can find out the status and list of all tickets by clicking on the button under the heading view all ticket on homepage

Technical Services:


What products can be covered in Technical Support?

All the products bought from our site are liable for technical support.

How long can it take?

We try to give a solution in the next 24hrs.

Are there any limits on tickets?

With valid entitlement, there are no limits on the number of questions/tickets, who asks the question, or the topic (as long as it is reasonably related to the product). There is a 16-hour limit on the effort we spend on an individual case before requiring a management review of the case.

Return/Replace:


What should I do if I get something else instead of my ordered accessory?

You can return or replace the item by raising a ticket with proper details of the requirement.

Can I return the product if I am not happy with it?

Products once sold cannot be returned. However, if a product is found defective it will be replaced.

What kind of warranty do you offer?

All products are covered by the manufacturer's 12-month warranty unless otherwise stated.
